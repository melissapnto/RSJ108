How to Enjoy Summer...

Assembly time: 3 months
Enjoyment rating: 100%

After a long tiring acdemic year it's important as journalism students to destress and have fun! Follow these steps to ensure you have a fun but relaxing summer to prepare you for the next semester.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Car
  • Phone
  • Friends (optional)
  • Books
  • Bed
  • Social Media

  1. Go to the Beach!

    With the sun out and Lake Tahoe only 30 minutes away its a given to go to the beach as much as possible over the summer. You can go with friends and enjoy the water or simply go on your own and have a little self-care moment. Especially after a long cold semester sitting in lecture halls all day, its important to get that vitamin D and enjoy the day!

  2. Read books

    The best way to better your writing is to reading. The more books you read the better, it will not only better your writing as a journalism but also give you ideas and references.It also keeps you occupied and entertained while being a relaxing activity.

  3. Stay caught up on the latest news

    As a Journalist it's important to stay in the loop of what is happening at all time. Especially during the summer when you aren't being told through professors you need to keep certain habits to ensure great journalism.

  4. Travel!

    Traveling with friends or family can always be a fun way to spend your time in the summer. Whether you stay close or go completely out of the country it still has the same effect. Traveling also help you see places and cultures you aren't used to which can really help with jounralistic writing.

  5. Sleep!

    Sleep is very important to your health and a great way to rejuvinate. The summer is the perfect time to catch up on all the sleep you lacked over the semester.
